What is Fire Emblem: Three Houses

Fire Emblem: Three Houses is a videogame published by Nintendo, and created by Intelligent Systems and Koei Tecmo. Intelligent Systems is well known for making all of the Fire Emblem games, Advanced Wars Series, and Paper Mario games. Fire Emblem: Three Houses came out July 26 of the year 2019. Fire Emblem: Three Houses is the 16th entry in the Fire Emblem series, and it is a game about three houses of students who attend an officers accademy, and the plot of the game will change depending on what house you pick. Fire Emblem is also a critically acclaimed game, and even won the Player's Voice Award at the 2019 Game Awards.
