A little about Fate/Stay Night

Fate/Stay Night is a popular visial novel series about a battle royal known as the "Holy Grail War" were mages summon "Heroic Spirits", the spirits of people from history and legends. The series is divided up into three different routes, and the story drastically changes each route you experience. The series has had quite a few adaptations made for it, such as a manga (Japanese Comic), multiple anime TV shows and movies, and even many spin-off games and stories too./p>
